We know you may have questions as you consider your options. Below are answers to common inquiries about programs, fees and tech life. An advisor can also help answer any other questions to help you choose the best fit for your goals.

Our program structure is very flexible and adaptive. The program runs from Monday to Friday and consists of physical and online classes with lectures from industry professionals. People with so many schedules can benefit from our programs.

We provide a diverse range of programs, covering areas such as software development, cybersecurity, data analysis, product design, and more. Explore our courses to find the perfect fit for your career goals.

Our tech school stands out due to its practical, hands-on approach to learning. We emphasize real-world applications, industry-relevant projects, and collaboration with professionals to ensure our graduates are well-prepared for the tech workforce.

Prerequisites vary depending on the program. However, we strive to make our courses accessible to individuals with a passion for technology. Check the specific program pages for detailed admission requirements.
